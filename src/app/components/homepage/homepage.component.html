Welcome to the ATHESA

Accessibility Through Empathy Simulation Application

ATHESA's Purpose

Those with disabilities can often be left out and unaccommodated for. ATHESA puts us in their shoes so that we can hopefully better understand their challenges, find solutions for them, and make the world an easier place to live in.

How to use ATHESA

Use the bar at the top to navigate between different rooms; each room will have items to interact with. Interact with the items normally, before using the bar at the side to simulate disabilities. Notice the difference!

Still Interested?
