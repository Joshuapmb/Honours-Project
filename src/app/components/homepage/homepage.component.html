Welcome to the ATHESA

Accessibility Through Empathy Simulation Application

ATHESA's Purpose

People with disabilities can often be unaccommodated for and misunderstood. ATHESA puts us in their shoes so that we can better understand their experiences and better empathise with them.

How to use ATHESA

Use the bar at the top to navigate between different environments. Each environment will have items to interact with. Interact with the items normally, before using the bar at the side to simulate disabilities. Notice the difference!

How to use ATHESA: The Office

Upon entering the office, your work phone will ring. Open the voicemail app and listen to the tasks - these are also listed on the top right. Now complete the tasks in whatever order you like. Once you are finished, go back onto the phone and use the email app to send your answers. Now use the button at the side of the page to simulate various disabilties and try the exercises again, think about which disabilities affect each exercise.

How to use ATHESA: The Living Room

Unlike the office, the living room takes a less structured approach to using ATHESA. There are still many items to interact with, including a television and stack of magazines which are especially impacted by colour blindness.

How to use ATHESA: Driving

This environment takes a gamified approach to understanding the experiences of people with disabilities. Instructions on how to drive are provided in the top right. Drive around the environment without disabilities without crashing. Once you complete the circuit successfully, try simulate different disabilities and see how they affect your performance.
